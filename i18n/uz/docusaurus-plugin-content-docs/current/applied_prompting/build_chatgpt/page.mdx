# GPT-3 dan ChatGPT yaratish



## Kirish

[ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com/chat) o'tgan oyda juda mashhur bo'ldi, atigi bir haftada million foydalanuvchini qo'lga kiritdi. Ajablanarlisi shundaki, asosiy model, GPT-3 2020-yilda taqdim etilgan va omma uchun <a href="https://openai.com/blog/api-no-waitlist/">bir yildan oshiq vaqt oldin ochilgan!</a>

Bilmaganlar uchun, ChatGPT bu OpenAI tomonidan yaratilgan yangi til modeli bo'lib, u GPT-3 asosida Fine-tuning qilinib, suhbat uchun optimallashtirilgan (@chatgpt2022). Unda foydalanuvchi uchun qulay chat interfeysi mavjud bo'lib, siz input kiritishingiz va AI yordamchisidan javob olishingiz mumkin. Uni [chat.openai.com](https://chat.openai.com/chat) saytida ko'rib chiqishingiz mumkin.

GPT-3 ning dastlabki versiyalari hozirgi GPT-3.5 seriyasidek rivojlangan bo'lmasa-da, baribir hayratlanarli edi. Ushbu modellardan API va <a href="https://beta.openai.com/playground">playground web UI interfeysi</a> orqali foydalanish mumkin bo'lib, u ayrim konfiguratsiya gipermetaparamentrlarini sozlash va promptlarni sinab ko'rish imkonini beradi. GPT-3
muhim darajada ommalashdi, biroq u ChatGPT darajasida tez tarqalmadi.

ChatGPT'ni GPT-3 bilan solishtirganda shu qadar muvaffaqiyatli qiladigan narsa shundaki, u oddiy odam uchun, ularning data science, language models yoki AI bo'yicha bilimidan qat'i nazar, sodda AI yordamchisi sifatida oson foydalanish imkoniyatini beradi.

Ushbu maqolada men ChatGPT kabi chatbotlarni GPT-3 kabi katta til modeli yordamida qanday amalga oshirish mumkinligini ko'rib chiqaman.

ChatGPT ham til modeli, ham foydalanuvchi interfeysi hisoblanadi. Foydalanuvchi interfeysiga kiritilgan prompt aslida foydalanuvchi va ChatGPT o'rtasidagi butun suhbatni o'z ichiga olgan kattaroq promptga joylanadi. Bu asosiy til modeliga suhbat kontekstini tushunish va mos ravishda javob berish imkonini beradi.



Til modeli promptni tugatadi, chunki u pre-training davomida o'rgangan ehtimolliklarga asoslanib, keyingi so'zlar qaysi bo'lishini aniqlaydi (@jurafsky2009).

GPT-3 oddiy ko'rsatma yoki promptdagi bir nechta misollardan "o'rganishi" mumkin. Ikkinchisi few-shot yoki in context learning deb ataladi (@brown2020language). Yuqoridagi chatbot promptida men Skippy nomli to'qima chatbot yarataman va unga foydalanuvchilarga javob berishni buyuraman. GPT-3 orqama-orqa formatini tushunadi, `USER: {user input}` va `SKIPPY: {skippy response}`. GPT-3 Skippy chatbot ekanligini va oldingi almashuvlar suhbat ekanligini tushunadi, shuning uchun keyingi foydalanuvchi kiritmasini berganimizda, "Skippy" javob beradi.

### Yodlash

Skippy va foydalanuvchi o'rtasidagi oldingi almashinuvlar keyingi prompt ga qo'shiladi. Har safar biz ko'proq foydalanuvchi kiritmasini beramiz va ko'proq chatbot javobini olamiz, prompt bu yangi almashinuvni o'z ichiga olgan holda kengayadi. Chatbot lar, masalan, Skippy va ChatGPT, **oldingi kiritmalarni eslab qolishi** shu tarzda amalga oshadi. Biroq, GPT-3 chatbot qancha ma'lumotni eslab qola olishi bo'yicha cheklov mavjud.

Promptlar bir necha marta almashilgandan so'ng juda katta bo'lishi mumkin, ayniqsa agar biz chatbotdan blog postlari kabi uzun javoblar yaratishni so'rasak. GPT-3 ga yuborilgan promptlar tokenlarga aylantiriladi, ya'ni alohida so'zlar yoki ularning qismlari. GPT-3 modellari, jumladan ChatGPT uchun, birlashtirilgan prompt va yaratilgan javob uchun <a href="https://help.openai.com/en/articles/4936856-what-are-tokens-and-how-to-count-them">4097 token (taxminan 3000 so'z)</a> cheklovi mavjud.

### Bir nechta misollar

Oldingi suhbatlarni saqlaydigan chatbot promptlarining ko'plab turli xil qo'llanilish holatlari mavjud. ChatGPT barcha maqsadlar uchun mo'ljallangan umumiy yordamchi sifatida yaratilgan va mening tajribamda, u kamdan-kam hollarda qo'shimcha savollar beradi.

#### Sizning kuningiz haqida so'raydigan terapiya chatboti

## Implementatsiya

Men Python dasturida oddiy GPT-3 asosidagi chatbotni qanday kodlashni ko'rsatib o'taman. Siz qurayotgan ilovaga GPT-3 ni qo'shish OpenAI API yordamida juda oson. Sizga OpenAI'da akkaunt yaratish va API kalitini olish kerak bo'ladi. Ularning hujjatlari bilan <a href="https://beta.openai.com/docs/introduction">bu yerda</a> tanishishingiz mumkin.

Nima qilishimiz kerakligi haqida umumiy ko'rinish:

1. Foydalanuvchi kiritgan matnni GPT-3 uchun chatbot promptiga formatlash
2. Chatbot javobini GPT-3 dan completion sifatida olish
3. Promptni foydalanuvchi kiritgan matn va chatbot javobi bilan yangilash
4. Loop

Mana men foydalanadigan prompt. Biz python yordamida \<conversation history\> va \<user input\> ni ularning haqiqiy qiymatlari bilan almashtirishimiz mumkin.

```python
chatbot_prompt = """
    As an advanced chatbot, your primary goal is to assist users to the best of your ability. This may involve answering questions, providing helpful information, or completing tasks based on user input. In order to effectively assist users, it is important to be detailed and thorough in your responses. Use examples and evidence to support your points and justify your recommendations or solutions.


<conversation history>

    User:
<user input>
    Chatbot:"""
```

Men keyingi foydalanuvchi kiritgan matn va oldingi suhbatni kuzatib boraman. Chatbot va foydalanuvchi o'rtasidagi yangi kirish/chiqish har bir aylanishda qo'shib boriladi.

```python
import openai

openai.api_key = "YOUR API KEY HERE"
model_engine = "text-davinci-003"
chatbot_prompt = """
As an advanced chatbot, your primary goal is to assist users to the best of your ability. This may involve answering questions, providing helpful information, or completing tasks based on user input. In order to effectively assist users, it is important to be detailed and thorough in your responses. Use examples and evidence to support your points and justify your recommendations or solutions.

<conversation history>

User:
<user input>
Chatbot:"""


def get_response(conversation_history, user_input):
    prompt = chatbot_prompt.replace("<conversation history>", conversation_history).replace("<user input>", user_input)

    # Get the response from GPT-3
    response = openai.Completion.create(
        engine=model_engine, prompt=prompt, max_tokens=2048, n=1, stop=None, temperature=0.5)

    # Extract the response from the response object
    response_text = response["choices"][0]["text"]

    chatbot_response = response_text.strip()

    return chatbot_response


def main():
    conversation_history = ""

    while True:
        user_input = input(">")
        if user_input == "exit":
            break
        chatbot_response = get_response(conversation_history, user_input)
        print(f"Chatbot: {chatbot_response}")
        conversation_history += f"User: {user_input}\nChatbot: {chatbot_response}\n"

main()
```
