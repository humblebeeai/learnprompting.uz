# Deformatsiyalangan Avlodlarni Tuzatish

Deformatsiyalangan avlodlar, ayniqsa inson tanasining qismlarida (masalan, qo'l, oyoq) ko'plab modellarda uchraydigan muammolardan biridir. Buni ma'lum darajada yaxshi negativ promptlar yordamida hal qilish mumkin (@blake2022with). Quyidagi misol [ushbu Reddit postidan](https://www.reddit.com/r/StableDiffusion/comments/z7salo/with_the_right_prompt_stable_diffusion_20_can_do/) moslashtirilgan.

## Misol

Stable Diffusion v1.5 va quyidagi promptdan foydalanib, biz Brad Pittning chiroyli suratini yaratamiz, albatta, qo'llaridan tashqari!


**Prompt:**

```text
studio medium portrait of Brad Pitt waving his hands, detailed, film, studio
  lighting, 90mm lens, by Martin Schoeller:6
```




Kuchli negativ promptdan foydalansak, ancha ishonarli qo'llarni yaratishimiz mumkin.


**Prompt:**

```text
studio medium portrait of Brad Pitt waving his hands, detailed, film, studio
  lighting, 90mm lens, by Martin Schoeller:6 | disfigured, deformed hands,
  blurry, grainy, broken, cross-eyed, undead, photoshopped, overexposed,
  underexposed, lowres, bad anatomy, bad hands, extra digits, fewer digits, bad
  digit, bad ears, bad eyes, bad face, cropped: -5
```



Shunga o'xshash negativ promptdan foydalanish boshqa tana qismlarida ham yordam berishi mumkin. Afsuski, bu usul doimiy natija bermaydi, shuning uchun yaxshi natijaga erishish uchun bir necha marta avlodlarni sinab ko'rishingiz kerak bo'ladi
yaxshi natijaga erishishdan oldin.
Kelajakda, bunday prompting keraksiz bo'ladi, chunki modellar yaxshilanadi.
Biroq, hozirda bu juda foydali usul hisoblanadi.
