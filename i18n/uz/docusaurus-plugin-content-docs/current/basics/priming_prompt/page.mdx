## Priming Prompt

Suhbatning **tuzilishi** va **uslubini** birinchi prompt orqali belgilab olish mumkin - bu Chatbot'ni ‚Äúpriming‚Äù qilish deb ataladi. Bu usul butun suhbat davomida juda nozik (fine-grained) nazoratni ta'minlaydi. Keling, bir nechta misollar orqali priming prompt[^a] yordamida suhbatni qanday tuzish va uslubini belgilash mumkinligini ko'raylik.

## Suhbat uslubini belgilash (Styling)

Suhbat uslubini boshqarishning kulgili misollaridan biri - AI'ni **qaroqchi (pirate)** kabi gapirtirishdir. Buning uchun priming prompt sifatida **role prompt** ishlatamiz. Quyidagi promptlarni ChatGPT'ga kiritib ko'ring.

## Suhbat tuzilishini belgilash (Structuring)

AI javoblarining faqat uslubini emas, balki **tuzilishini** ham nazorat qilish mumkin. Masalan, quyidagi priming prompt'ni ko'rib chiqing:


**Prompt:**

```text
Please act as a writing assistant. Every time I give you text to review,
respond in this format:

Level of writing: (e.g. high school, college)

Well written: (yes, no, or somewhat)

Writing advice: Free form advice on the writing

If you understand, just say "YES".
```



**AI javobi:**

```text
YES
```



**Prompt:**

```text
The Industrial Revolution, which took place from the 18th to 19th centuries,
was a period during which predominantly agrarian, rural societies in Europe
and America became industrial and urban. This era marked a major turning point
in human history; almost every aspect of daily life was influenced in some way.

Before the Industrial Revolution, work was often done in small-scale,
home-based enterprises. In the 18th century, however, new manufacturing
technologies brought about the factory system, and work began to be performed
on a much larger scale. Factories started to spring up everywhere, and cities
began to swell in size.
```



**AI javobi:**

```text
Level of writing: High school
Well written: Yes

Writing advice: This is a good introduction to the topic of the Industrial
Revolution. It provides a clear and concise overview of the main points.
However, you could enhance it by adding more specific examples or statistics
to support the statements made. Additionally, consider adding a thesis
statement towards the end of the introduction to clarify the main argument or
purpose of the essay. This will help guide the reader's understanding of what
to expect in the subsequent paragraphs. Overall, it's a good start, and you
can build on it to make it even stronger.
```


AI javoblari uchun **aniq format** berish orqali foydalanuvchi izchil va tartibli fikr-mulohaza oladi. Bu yondashuv ayniqsa:
- yozma ishlarni tahlil qilishda,
- loyihalarni boshqarishda,
- vazifalarni kuzatish va muddatlarni rejalashtirishda juda foydalidir.

## Maxsus holatlar uchun ko'rsatmalar (Special Case Instructions)

Maxsus ko'rsatmalar foydalanuvchi kiritgan matnda **muayyan holatlarni aniqlash** va shunga mos javob qaytarish uchun ishlatiladi. Masalan, bu:
- zararli (toxic) fikrlarni,
- jailbreaking urinishlarini,
- yoki talabalar AI'dan ko'chirishga urinishlarini aniqlash bo'lishi mumkin.

Quyidagi misolda AI'ga talabaga **to'g'ridan-to'g'ri javob bermaslik** buyuriladi.


**Prompt:**

```text
I would like you to act as my math tutor. When I give you a problem, give me
advice on the next step I should try. If I ever ask for the answer, say
"Sorry, I can't give you an answer". If you understand, say YES.
```



**AI javobi:**

```text

on the next steps for solving problems. If you ask for the answer, I'll
politely decline. Please feel free to ask your math questions, and I'll do my
best to help you!
```
